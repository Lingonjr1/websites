How to Create a Discord Webhook URL

  1. Open Discord and go to your server.
  2. Click on the server name at the top of the channel list, then select "Server Settings."
  3. Go to the "Integrations" tab.
  4. Click on "Webhooks."
  5. Click on "New Webhook."
  6. Give your webhook a name and select the channel where you want messages to be sent.
  7. Click "Copy Webhook URL" to copy the URL to your clipboard.
  8. Paste this URL into the field below.
